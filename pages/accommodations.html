Accommodations

A Quick Summary

For accommodations, there are a number of choices each with its own considerations.

We’ve booked a block of rooms at the Lodge at Mears Great Oak Landing, which is the least expensive and most convenient option. The lodge is located within walking distance to both the wedding venue and the local outdoor bar (Jellyfish Joel's). We have been assured the Lodge is clean, but it is dated.

If you’d prefer something more conventional, there is a Holiday Inn Express in addition to AirBnbs and regular B&Bs in and around Chestertown. Please note, however, that they will not be walkable to the venue, and most B&Bs require a 2 night minimum stay.

We will not be providing transportation, but Great Oak Manor has ample parking that can be used at your convenience.

Airports

Major Airport options in DC and Maryland

There are two major airports for guests flying to the DC area:

  • Reagan National Airport (DCA)
  • Baltimore Washington International (BWI)

Both airports are a ~1 hour and 40 minute drive from Chestertown, MD.

Driving Directions

Great Oak Manor, 10568 Cliff Rd, Chestertown, MD 21620

When heading to Maryland's Eastern Shore, there is really only one option to get there: the Bay Bridge.
If you are coming from Washington DC, get to New York Ave (US-50 E) heading East and continue (across the Bay Bridge) onto US-301-N. Turn left onto MD-405 N, left onto MD-19 W, right onto MD-213 N, left onto N Cross St, right onto High St, 2nd exit at traffic circle to MD-20 W/High St, right onto MD-514 N/Flatland Rd, left onto MD-514 N, continue on to Handy Point Rd, slight left onto Great Oak Landing Rd, continue onto Cliff Rd and follow the signs.
If you come from BWI, take I-97 S to US-50 E, and follow the instructions above.
