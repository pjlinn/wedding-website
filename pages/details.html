Wedding-eve Details

Guests coming to Chestertown Friday are invited to Jellyfish Joels for celebratory drinks and a hearty welcome. The wedding party should arrive by 8:00 PM and the attire is casual (it's a beach bar).

Address:

22170 Great Oak Landing Rd, Chestertown, MD 21620

Saturday

Wedding Day Details

Ceremony: 5:00 PM (outdoor)
Cocktail Hour: 5:30 PM (outdoor)
Seated Dinner: 6:30 PM (outdoor/tented)

First Course: Summer Salad with Pears

Main Course: Crab Cakes and Beef Tenderloin with corn and tomato “salsa,” pan roasted potatoes, and local green beans

Kids Option: Grilled cheese, french fries (Old Bay) and tomato soup

Dessert: Lemon-raspberry, chocolate, and strawberry cake

Post-Reception After-Party:
There will be an after-party in the Great Oak Atrium immediately following a send-off of the bride and groom (~10 PM). The after-party will include late night food, self-serve adult beverages, and a DJ set by the newly minted Mr. and Mrs. DJ Pool Party -- among other things (guy vs. girl flip cup anyone?).

All guests are encouraged to continue celebrating with us as long as they wish.

Venue: Great Oak Manor
Address: 10568 Cliff Rd, Chestertown, MD 21620
Attire: Black Tie Optional
